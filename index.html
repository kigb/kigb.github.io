About Me

I am a master student in Master of Intelligent Information Systems (MIIS) at Carnegie Mellon University, Language Technologies Institute, directed by Prof. Ameet Talwalkar. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, majoring in Software Engineering, guided by Prof. Xiaodong Gu.

I am interested in both research and engineering. I previously interned at Ant Group (OceanBase) and StepFun AI, and co-founded Emagen AI. This summer, I will join Jump Trading as a research intern.

My research focuses on LLM Agents, spanning both application and evaluation.
